\section{Strengths, Limitations, and Extensions}

The v1.0 demo has three strengths. First, it connects evaluation to planning instead of stopping at supplier ranking. Second, it generates a reproducible table and figure. Third, it records outputs in stable locations for later review.

The limitations are also clear. The data are synthetic, the planning algorithm is greedy, the weights are illustrative, and demand uncertainty is ignored. A stronger version should use real supplier history, entropy or AHP weighting, mixed-integer programming, carrier allocation, and scenario stress tests.
