\section{Assumptions}

\begin{enumerate}
  \item Weekly demand is known and deterministic in this demo.
  \item Supplier capacity, price, loss rate, and reliability are constant over the eight-week horizon.
  \item Materials from different suppliers are interchangeable after delivery.
  \item Transport loss is proportional to the ordered quantity.
  \item A feasible plan is acceptable for v1.0 even if it is not globally cost-optimal.
\end{enumerate}
