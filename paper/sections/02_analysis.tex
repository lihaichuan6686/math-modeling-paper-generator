\section{Problem Analysis}

This problem is a small supply-planning task. It has the same skeleton as many CUMCM supply-chain papers, but with fewer suppliers and a shorter planning horizon. The appropriate v1.0 route is:

\begin{center}
\texttt{supplier evaluation -> greedy planning baseline -> inventory validation}
\end{center}

The evaluation step ranks suppliers by price, loss rate, and reliability. The planning step uses the ranking to allocate weekly orders while preserving a minimum ending inventory. The validation step checks whether the generated plan meets the safety inventory target in every week.

This route is intentionally simple. Its purpose is to verify the full toolchain from problem intake to generated artifacts and paper review.
