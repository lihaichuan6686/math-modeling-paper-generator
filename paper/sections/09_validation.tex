\section{Validation and Sensitivity Analysis}

The minimum ending inventory in the generated plan is 15 units, so the safety inventory constraint is satisfied in all eight weeks. This is a feasibility validation rather than a full robustness analysis.

The main sensitivity risk is the fixed weighting rule in the supplier-evaluation score. If the reliability weight or loss-rate weight changes, supplier ordering may change. A real CUMCM paper should test multiple weighting schemes and compare the resulting cost, inventory, and supplier composition.
