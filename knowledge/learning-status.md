## Maintenance Rule

When a new paper, template, or note becomes important, convert it into one of:

- a route rule,
- an algorithm card,
- a LaTeX contract,
- a quality rule,
- a prompt,
- or a runbook update.

Do not let important reading stay only in raw source form.
